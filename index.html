What you'll do

Every product hides a creature

Real barcodes become collectible beasts. Real nutrition becomes their power.

Scan & capture

Point your camera at any food barcode. A unique beast is generated — its element and stats derived from the product's real nutritional values.

Evolve your beasts

Scan the same product again to level up a beast through evolution stages, boosting HP, defense and unlocking abilities.

Live duels

Battle a friend in the same room over WiFi or Bluetooth — no internet needed. Turn-synced, host-authoritative, cheat-proof.

Online arena

Jump into random matchmaking or challenge friends online. Deterministic lockstep engine keeps every match fair.

Friends & trading

Add friends with a code or QR, then trade beast cards. Symmetric offers, instant sync, notifications even when the app is closed.

Global leaderboard

Climb the online Top 100 ranked by total XP. Every capture, evolution and victory pushes you up the board.

Missions & trophies

Fresh daily missions, parallel weekly events, and permanent trophies to chase. Earn coins for the shop as you play.

Health corner

Every beast carries the real Nutri-Score and macros of the food it came from — a battler that quietly teaches you what you eat.
